Elastic Beanstalk:
- How can I quickly get my application into the cloud? 
- How can I get the entire environment ready?
- Platform as a service: platform is created so that you can put your
	code over the system. 
- Quick deployment of your applications. 
- Reduce management complexity. 
- Also supports a large range of platforms. Go, Java, Python, Ruby, Node.js, 
	etc. 
- The only you need you have is to create your code. 
- Steps to deploy and update are based only on the creation of your 
	application. 
- Demonstration: 
	- You don't even need an application. You can select
	an example one to play around with beanstalk.
